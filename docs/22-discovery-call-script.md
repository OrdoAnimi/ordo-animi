# Discovery Call Notes

Version: 0.1

Use this guide for early VALOUR conversations.

The goal is to understand whether architecture leaders recognise the problem and whether scenario rehearsal would help.

Start by asking about the person's role, team context, common leadership conversations, and the moments where architects struggle to influence decisions.

Then ask which situations matter most: review boards, executive briefings, workshops, stakeholder conflict, risk discussions, delivery pressure, trade-off explanations, strategy translation, first meetings with new teams, or workday handover.

Close by asking whether they would review the pilot pack, try one scenario, or suggest another contact.

After the call, record the main pain, most valuable scenario, user fit, concerns, and next action.
