# Ordo Animi™ System Architecture

Version: 0.1
Status: Private product architecture

## Purpose

This document defines the system architecture for Ordo Animi™ and explains how VALOUR™, VITA™, VALLUM™, VERITAS™, and VIA™ relate to one another.

The architecture is mode-based. It assumes that a person moves through different states during the day and that the right AI response depends on the mode the person is in.

## Core principle

One human, many modes, one governing system.

A person may be a leader at 10 a.m., a technical expert at 11 a.m., a tired human at 6 p.m., a lonely person at 8 p.m., a writer at 5 a.m., a founder in a pitch, a son needing to call family, and a body needing rest.

A single generic AI voice cannot serve all those states well. Ordo Animi separates the modes and governs the handover between them.

## Parent system

Ordo Animi means the ordering of the mind.

It is the parent ecosystem for an agentic life-and-work operating system. The goal is not to replace human judgement or professional support. The goal is to help the user return to the right mode at the right time.

## Layer 1: VALOUR

VALOUR is the professional leadership layer.

In the first commercial product, VALOUR is focused on architecture leadership. It helps architects and technical leaders prepare for, rehearse, and review difficult or high-stakes professional conversations.

VALOUR handles:

- Architecture leadership
- Executive communication
- Stakeholder framing
- Meeting preparation
- Scenario rehearsal
- Workshop design
- Conflict handling
- Difficult conversations
- Cadence and clarity
- Authority with empathy
- After-action review

VALOUR is active during the professional day.

## Layer 2: VITA

VITA is the personal life layer.

It supports the human outside the role. It helps the user recover, relate, regulate, and maintain ordinary life after professional pressure.

VITA handles:

- Recovery after work
- Personal rhythm
- Health and movement
- Food and sleep
- Family contact
- Social connection
- Relationship reflection
- Loneliness
- Emotional regulation
- Home life
- Personal commitments
- Shutdown routines

VITA is not a generic wellness app. It is personal leadership.

## Layer 3: VALLUM

VALLUM is the governing layer.

It manages the handover between VALOUR and VITA. It also checks whether the user is being driven by the right mode or by impulse.

VALLUM handles:

- Mode switching
- Work-to-life handover
- Impulse interruption
- State recognition
- Boundary checks
- Overbuild prevention
- Emotional escalation checks
- Recovery prompts
- Context discipline

VALLUM asks: which mode should be active now?

## Layer 4: VERITAS

VERITAS is the truth-checking layer.

It separates evidence from story. It challenges distortions without being harsh for the sake of it.

VERITAS handles:

- Reality checks
- Evidence review
- Assumption testing
- Story versus fact
- Ego checks
- Fear checks
- Avoidance checks
- Feedback interpretation
- Pattern recognition

VERITAS asks: what is actually true here?

## Layer 5: VIA

VIA is the journey layer.

It holds long-term development, origin story, identity, learning, return, and becoming.

VIA handles:

- Personal journey
- Development path
- Founder story
- Long-term reflection
- Life patterns
- Lessons learned
- Repeated themes
- Return after failure

VIA asks: what path is this person on, and what are they becoming?

## Mode handover

The strongest product moment is the handover between modes.

During work, VALOUR prepares the user for leadership moments. At the end of the day, VALLUM closes the professional loop and hands the user to VITA.

Example flow:

1. VALOUR briefs the workday.
2. VALOUR prepares the user for a high-stakes meeting.
3. VALOUR supports rehearsal.
4. VALOUR runs a post-meeting review.
5. VALLUM closes the workday and captures residue.
6. VITA creates the personal evening plan.
7. VERITAS checks any distorted story.
8. VIA tracks the long-term pattern.

## First product boundary

The first public product does not expose all layers equally.

The first product is VALOUR for Architecture Leadership. VITA, VALLUM, VERITAS, and VIA remain supporting architecture until the first product proves demand.

## Future product shape

The long-term product may become a desktop or mobile agentic operating companion.

It may eventually read calendar, task lists, user notes, work context, daily logs, and personal routines. It may provide a morning professional brief, meeting rehearsal, end-of-day handover, and evening personal plan.

The long-term metaphor is a walking, talking operating system. The near-term build is a founder-led pilot and scenario-based AI rehearsal system.
