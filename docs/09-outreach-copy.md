## Purpose

This document provides early outreach copy for finding pilot users, friendly testers, funders, and architecture leaders who can help validate VALOUR.

## Positioning rule

Lead with VALOUR, not the full Ordo Animi system.

The first audience needs to understand one clear problem: architects and technical leaders need help preparing for difficult leadership conversations before they happen.

## Short LinkedIn post

I am piloting a new AI-assisted leadership rehearsal system for architects and technical leaders.

The idea is simple: architecture often succeeds or fails in the conversation before it becomes a document.

Architects are expected to brief executives, challenge poor solutions, run workshops, handle delivery conflict, explain trade-offs, and influence decisions without always having formal authority.

VALOUR helps users prepare for those moments before they happen.

I am looking for a small number of early users or one small architecture team to test the first pilot.

If you are an architect, technical leader, practice lead, or someone responsible for improving architecture capability, I would value a conversation.

## Discovery call opener

The problem I am testing is simple.

Architects are often expected to lead decisions without being trained for the leadership conversations that make those decisions possible.

I am not testing whether architects need more frameworks. I am testing whether they need better preparation for the moments where they must brief, challenge, explain, influence, and recover.

## Discovery questions

Where do architects in your environment most often lose trust or influence?

Which conversations are hardest for architecture teams?

Do your architects struggle more with executives, delivery teams, security, business stakeholders, or governance forums?

How do you currently train architects for stakeholder communication and difficult conversations?

Would scenario rehearsal before real meetings be useful?

Who would benefit first from a pilot?

What would make this worth paying for?
