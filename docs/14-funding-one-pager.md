# VALOUR Funding One-Pager

Version: 0.1
Status: Private one-page funding narrative

## Product

VALOUR is an AI architectural leadership copilot.

It helps architects and technical leaders prepare for, rehearse, and review the conversations that shape major technology decisions.

## Parent system

VALOUR is the first commercial product from Ordo Animi, a broader agentic life-and-work operating system.

Ordo Animi includes five layers: VALOUR for professional leadership, VITA for personal life and recovery, VALLUM for mode governance, VERITAS for truth checking, and VIA for long-term journey.

## Problem

Architects are trained in frameworks, diagrams, artefacts, cloud, governance, and technical methods.

But architecture leadership often succeeds or fails in conversation.

Architects must brief executives, guide workshops, challenge poor decisions, explain risk, handle delivery pressure, manage stakeholder conflict, and translate complexity into decisions.

Many are not trained for that leadership layer.

## Solution

VALOUR gives architects a practical rehearsal and reflection system.

The user brings a real upcoming leadership moment.

VALOUR helps them prepare the frame, rehearse the conversation, refine the language, then review the real outcome afterward.

The product loop is prepare, rehearse, perform, review, and improve.

## First wedge

Architecture leadership.

Initial users include enterprise architects, solution architects, cloud architects, security architects, principal consultants, architecture practice leads, and technical leaders moving into advisory roles.

## First pilot

VALOUR Architecture Leadership Pilot.

A four-week founder-led pilot using scenario templates, AI rehearsal, and after-action review.

Initial scenario examples include architecture review board, executive briefing, discovery workshop, challenging a poor solution, risk pushback, delivery conflict, trade-off explanation, strategy-to-architecture translation, first meeting with a new team, and end-of-day handover into VITA.

## Validation ask

Seeking a small validation round to test the pilot, refine the scenario library, build lightweight public assets, and gather user evidence.

Indicative validation range: AUD 10,000 to AUD 25,000.

This is not a full platform build. It is a demand validation round.

## 90-day validation target

Complete pilot materials, recruit initial pilot users, deliver the pilot cycle, capture feedback, produce testimonials where appropriate, refine the offer and pricing, and decide whether to build the digital MVP.

## Why this founder

The founder has deep lived experience in enterprise architecture, cloud, advisory, delivery, stakeholder engagement, and complex technology decision-making.

The product emerged from real use of AI as a professional and personal operating system before being formalised into Ordo Animi.

## Why now

AI makes rehearsal, feedback, and personalised preparation easier to deliver at lower cost.

Architecture teams need stronger communication and influence capability as technology decisions become more complex, cross-functional, and business-critical.

## Core statement

VALOUR helps architects become trusted leaders before the meeting decides their credibility for them.
