## Template 3: AI rehearsal setup

Use this template to start a rehearsal session.

I am preparing for the following architecture leadership scenario:

[insert scenario]

The audience is:

[insert audience]

The outcome I need is:

[insert outcome]

The risk is:

[insert risk]

Act as the stakeholder group. Ask one question at a time. Challenge my clarity, cadence, empathy, authority, and decision framing. After each answer, give concise feedback and suggest a stronger version.

## Operating principle

Do not overcomplicate the pilot.

One user. One real leadership moment. One rehearsal. One after-action review. One improvement pattern.

Repeat until the product proves itself.
