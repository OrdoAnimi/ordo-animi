# After-Action Review Template

## User story

As a VALOUR pilot user, I want to review what happened after the real or simulated conversation so that I can turn the experience into learning.

## Acceptance criteria

- Real or simulated outcome is captured.
- What worked is captured.
- What did not work is captured.
- Confidence after VALOUR is recorded.
- One improvement focus is identified.

## Success criteria

The review is successful when the user can name what changed, what still needs work, and what they would do differently next time.
