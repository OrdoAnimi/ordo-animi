# Stage 02 — Scenario Selection

## User ID

## Scenario selected

VALOUR-S04: Executive Briefing

## Variant

Cloud migration investment decision for CFO and CIO — recommending lift-and-shift versus re-platform with financial risk framing.

## Fit score

9 out of 10. The user's situation maps directly to the executive briefing scenario. The CFO/CIO audience, the investment decision frame, and the user's explicit awareness of the translation gap are all strong indicators.

## Scenario description

The executive briefing scenario prepares a technical practitioner to translate a complex technical recommendation into a concise, decision-ready executive communication. The focus is on financial and business consequence language, not architecture detail. The practitioner must establish the recommendation first, then support it with risk, investment, and outcome language.

## Key challenges for this variant

- CFO and CIO have different priorities. The brief must address both without splitting focus.
- The migration decision is technically complex but must be presented as a binary choice with managed risk.
- The user must resist the temptation to justify the recommendation with architecture detail.
- The closing position must be actionable: a clear recommendation with a named next step.

## Scenario fit rationale

The user has identified the core gap: technical clarity without executive language. The VALOUR executive briefing scenario is designed specifically to help technically strong practitioners reframe their expertise in business and financial consequence terms. The fit score reflects the direct match between the user's situation and the scenario design.
