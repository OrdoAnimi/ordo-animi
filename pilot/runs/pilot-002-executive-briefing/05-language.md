## Decision version

The recommendation is to proceed with the managed migration. The biggest risk is timeline, and we manage it through a phased delivery model with a hard decision gate at month six. If the programme is not on track at that gate, you have a clean option to pause without having committed the full investment. This is a controlled investment decision, not an open-ended commitment. Decision version was most useful — led with recommendation, consequence, and risk mitigation. Executive version was second.
