# Ordo Animi Charter

## Mission

Primary user-facing intelligence platform and Valour product home.

## Owned Capabilities

- Ordo Animi platform experience
- Valour
- Custos
- guided workflows
- AI-assisted user interaction
- product orchestration
- flagship production surface

## Explicit Exclusions

- estate constitution
- central policy registry
- client archive authority
- all future products
- unrelated product code

## Accountable Owner

Founder / CTO.

## Upstream Relationships

- magister-automatorum
- imperium
- mercatus
- lex
- civitas
- memoria
- fabrica
- forma

## Downstream Relationships

- Archivum
- probatio
- specula
- nexus

## Authority Boundaries

Authoritative for the primary user experience and Valour product surface. It must not absorb responsibilities assigned to other repositories.

## Stop Conditions

Stop and request Founder / CTO routing if a change would cross repository boundaries, introduce runtime implementation during v0.1, add credentials, claim deployment or assurance without evidence, or alter institutional ownership.

## Criteria For Future Implementation

Future implementation requires approved scope, evidence of need, repository-boundary review, security review, data classification, and explicit release acceptance.
